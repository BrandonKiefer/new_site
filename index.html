Projects

MyBikeDatabase is my capstone project and was an idea that I had before I even started coding. As a DIY cyclist, I have assembled and repaired a variety of single-speed biycles many with entirely individual custom-ordered parts. A problem that I had for the longest time was actually keeping track of the specs for all of my bikes' various components-- for which there are SO many. If you're familiar with bikes at all, you may already know the dread of having to disassemble parts of your bike to check something like the length of a spoke or the diameter of your steering tube if you're looking to make an upgrade or a repair. The last thing you want is waste your time and money purchasing an incompatible part. MyBikeDatabase allows users of any background to begin recording the specs of their bikes, for as long as they have them, streamlining all futures repairs and upgrades!

Games

SNAKE

If you grew up in the 90s, you've probably played this game before-- probably on one of your old flip phones. Remember those? Well now you can relive the good ole days of dumb flip phones right here in you browser! You start off as a baby snake and go around eating apples to grow and improve your score but be careful not to eat your own tail!

TRAILS

This is based on the classic game that came with Windows 3.x operating systems. While similar to snake, and actually more like Tron, the object of the game is to battle your computer-opponent in a competetion for space as you try to force them to collide into your trail, the edge of the screen, or their own trail. Surprisingly, I couldn't find a playable version online so I made my own that is playable in the browser! I think it's still pretty addictive so I hope you enjoy!
